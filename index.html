2. Grammar Masterclass (جميع القواعد)

Rule Structure & Usage Example
Rule 1 Wishes I wish + Past Simple (Present regret)
I wish + Past Perfect (Past regret)		 I wish corruption disappeared.
Rule 2 High Time It's high time + Subject + Past Simple It's high time we fought fraud.
Rule 3 Advice Had better + Stem (Verb without to) You had better not buy fake goods.
Rule 4 So/Such...that So + Adj + That / Such + Noun Phrase + That It is such a serious crime that we must act.
Rule 5 Condition Provided that / As long as + Present, Future As long as we work hard, we will win.

3. Model Essays (وضعيات إدماجية)

Topic 1: How to Eradicate Corruption? (كيف نقضي على الفساد)

Corruption is a cancer. To fight it, governments must pass strict laws to punish corrupt people. Moreover, we need to promote transparency and accountability in all sectors. Finally, education plays a vital role in teaching the new generation moral values.

Topic 2: The Dangers of Counterfeiting (مخاطر التقليد)

Counterfeiting is not just a crime; it is a threat to our health. First, fake medicines can kill people. Second, it destroys the national economy by reducing taxes. Consumers should be aware and refuse to buy fake brands.

Topic 3: Ethics in Business (الأخلاقيات في العمل)

Business ethics means following a code of conduct. Honest companies attract more customers and gain a good reputation. When a company is ethical, it respects workers' rights and protects the environment, leading to long-term success.

Topic 4: Child Labor (عمالة الأطفال)

Child labor is an unethical practice that deprives children of their rights. Many companies exploit children in factories because they are cheap. It is high time the international community protected children and sent them back to schools.
